Who We Are

Forged From Many, Standing as One

The Brotherhood of Blended Bloodlines Committee (BBBC) was founded in 2009 in Detroit, Michigan by a coalition of multiracial families, civil rights attorneys, and community organizers who refused to check a single box. We are a 501(c)(4) political advocacy organization and community network dedicated to the advancement of hybrid and mixed-heritage peoples across the United States and abroad.

  • Legislative advocacy for multiracial recognition in federal census and identity law
  • Community programs supporting mixed-heritage youth and families
  • Political education and voter mobilization across blended communities
  • Legal aid and anti-discrimination defense for hybrid individuals
  • Cultural preservation and celebration of blended heritage
  • Armed self-defense and community protection through our Defense Corps
Paramilitary Wing

BBBC Defense Corps

The BBBC Defense Corps is the protective and paramilitary arm of the Brotherhood, formed in response to rising hate crimes targeting multiracial individuals and families. Operating within applicable federal and state law, the Corps provides community security, self-defense training, armed escort, and rapid-response protection for members under threat.

The Corps is organized into regional chapters, each commanded by a Chapter Marshal. Membership is open to BBBC members in good standing who complete our 12-week training program.
